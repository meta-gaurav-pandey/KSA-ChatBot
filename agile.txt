**************************Agile
* The older convestional model such as Waterfall model , had a lot of flaws hence agile comes into the picture
* It is the practice followed to continous iteration of the developement and testing in SDLC
* Break the projects into smaller chunks so, client will be able to see the progress of projects constantly
* More adaptable to chaging requirement 
* Encourage the F2F communication , teamwork and constant feedback from the end users


******Scrum
* Popular apporch to implement the Agile methodology
* Help users to manage the software developement with iterative method 
* Regular and repeatable work cycle in Scrum during which work is completed and made ready for reviews 
  is known as Scrum sprint
*  Sprint hardly last for 1-2 weeks and for next sprint plan is made team workers
